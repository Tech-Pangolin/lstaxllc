About the Owner

I'm passionate about getting your taxes done right

Cedric Obiaka, is a seasoned tax professional and owner of L&S TAX LLC, with six+ years of experience in the tax industry. He has been dedicated to providing professional tax services to individuals and businesses alike.

Throughout his career, Cedric has honed his expertise in various aspects of tax preparation, including individual tax returns, small business taxes, tax planning strategies, and sales and use tax compliance. He has consistently helped his clients save money and maximize their tax returns by staying updated on the latest tax laws and utilizing innovative tax-saving techniques.

Today, Cedric's passion for the industry and his commitment to delivering exceptional service have led him to expand by officially starting L&S TAX LLC. As the firm grows with new clients and new areas of taxation to offer, Cedric will be actively seeking talented individuals who share his dedication to providing outstanding tax services and helping clients achieve their financial goals.
